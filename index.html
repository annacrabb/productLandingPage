About Us

Whenever a die is lost, it actually ends up with us. Just like bobby pins, pen caps, and your hopes and dreams, the lost ones have to end up somewhere. We're not sure why it happens, and it makes keeping track of inventory quite the challenge, but it's also why we are one of the most eco-friendly dice manufacturers around. So cash in on all of the lost items you've been offering karmically into the universe and take advantage of our rock-bottom prices!

As new sets arrive in our warehouse, our associates sort them by vibe, matching them up with 5e's playable races and classes. When you place your order, you'll be prompted to select the race and class of the PC you're looking to equip. One of our associates will compile a complete or mostly complete set of dice from our current selection.

Dice
